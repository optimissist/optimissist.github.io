Black Spire Outpost Obelisk

The Black Spire Outpost Obelisk was a stone obelisk located in Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu. It was carved by local artisans with patterns matching those found on the outpost's original buildings. It is a tradition amongst visitors and locals of the outpost to touch the obelisk and say "Til the Spire" for good luck and in hopes of safely returning to the outpost in future

Seezelslak's Cantina

Seezelslak's Cantina is a cantina in Batuu, located near a spaceport in Black Spire Outpost and owned by Seezelslak. The cantina serves drinks, and had a jukebox and a Repulsor Darts board. Stop by for one of Seezelslak's famous stories, you may even hear about Yoda and Padawan Ady Sun'Zee.
